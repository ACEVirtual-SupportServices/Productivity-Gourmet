Privacy Policy

How I collect, use, and protect your information

Effective Date: February 16, 2026

Productivity Gourmet respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit this website or enquire about services.

Information I Collect

Information You Provide

When you contact me or enquire about services, I may collect:

  • Name and contact information (email address)
  • Company or business name
  • Details about your enquiry or service needs
  • Any other information you choose to share in communications

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit this website, the following may be collected automatically:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • IP address and general location data
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site

How I Use Your Information

Information collected is used to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and service requests
  • Provide and improve services
  • Communicate with you about your engagement
  • Understand how visitors use the website
  • Comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing

Your personal information is never sold, rented, or traded to third parties. Information may be shared only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services that help operate this website (e.g., Cloudflare Pages for hosting, Tally for form submissions)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect legal rights
  • Business Transfers: In connection with any merger, sale, or transfer of the business

Data Security

Appropriate technical and organisational measures are implemented to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. No internet transmission is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information held about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

Cookies

This website currently does not use cookies beyond those set by Google Analytics for anonymous traffic measurement. If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.

Third-Party Links

This website may contain links to third-party websites. Productivity Gourmet is not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites. Reviewing their privacy policies before providing any personal information is recommended.

Children's Privacy

Services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. Personal information from children is not knowingly collected.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be communicated by posting the updated policy on this page and updating the Effective Date above.
